The Predator

The Predator, also known as the Yautja,typically range from 7 to 8 feet tall (approximately 2.13 to 2.44 meters) and possess a muscular, imposing physique. They have distinctive features, including mandibles, a reptilian skin texture, and a crest on their heads. One of the Predator's most notable abilities is its advanced cloaking technology After a successful hunt, Predators often take trophies from their kills, such as skulls or spines, which they use to showcase their prowess.

The Face Hugger

The Facehugger is a small, spider-like creature with long, flexible limbs and a distinct, oval-shaped body. Its most notable feature is its elongated tail, which it uses to secure itself to its host. The Facehugger implants a Xenomorph embryo into a host organism. If you try to cut the face hugger then be careful as it posseses acidic blood.

The xenomorph

Xenomorphs are known for their skeletal, humanoid frame, elongated skulls, and a double set of jaws.Their blood is highly corrosive acid, which poses a significant danger to anyone attempting to harm them. Xenomorphs exhibit rudimentary intelligence and are driven primarily by instinct to hunt and propagate their species. They are known for their stealth and ability to ambush prey. They often operate in packs or hives, led by a Queen who oversees reproduction and hive dynamics.
